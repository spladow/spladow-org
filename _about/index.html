Hi, I’m Thomas. I’ve written for Filter, Tiny Mix Tapes, Arrested Motion, Examiner, and The Art Street Journal, as well as a few other sites. Not for a long time though. If you’re looking to read my thoughts, this blog and my Twitter are your best bets.

I live in Seattle and work at Nokia. I’m proud to be a former member of the amazingly talented Border Stylo and spire.io teams.

If you want to talk about anything you read here, or just talk in general, feel free to send me an email.
